The problem

Tech Skool needed to understand how competitors were winning on social media — what content formats, community tactics, and brand strategies were driving engagement in the Nigerian edtech space — in order to carve out a distinct and defensible position.

What I did

  • Audited 3 Nigerian edtech brands across Instagram, LinkedIn and Facebook
  • Tracked content types, posting frequency, engagement rates, follower counts, tone and format performance
  • Conducted a SWOT analysis for each competitor
  • Synthesised findings into strategic recommendations

Key findings

  • Community-driven content outperformed other formats by 40%
  • Competitors rely on paid influencer reach to compensate for weak organic pull
  • No competitor owns a consistent, instructor-led brand voice
  • Incentive-driven audiences have low retention — an opportunity for loyalty-led positioning

Strategic opportunities identified

  • Win on organic community trust instead of influencer spend
  • Own a strong brand voice — no competitor has one
  • Position as the platform people choose, not the one they showed up to for a stipend

Impact

Findings directly informed Tech Skool's content calendar and social media posting strategy, shaping editorial direction for subsequent campaigns.
